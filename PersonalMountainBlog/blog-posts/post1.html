  • Mount Damavand

    Mount Damavand is located in western Iran in the Zacros mountain range and is the country's highest peak, reaching about 5,610 metres (18,405 ft) above sea level, making it the second highest peak in the Middle East.

    Terrain and Climate:
    The terrain of Mount Dmound is characterized by high-rise slopes and deep valleys, located in an area rich in spectacular landscapes and scenic mountain views. Its climate is very cold during winter and mild summer.

    Tourism & Activities:
    Mount Damound is a major tourist destination for nature lovers and adventurers, where visitors can enjoy climbing, mountain trips, mountain biking and camping. It also offers opportunities to see the spectacular landscape, enjoy fresh air and charming nature.

    Culture and History:
    Mount Demound occupies an important place in Iranian culture and history, as it is a national symbol of determination and will. Iranian mythology and popular stories refer to his prominent role in Iranian culture and heritage.

    Environmental conservation and sustainability:
    Officials and the community pay great attention to protecting Mount Dumound's environment and preserving the biodiversity of the area, by implementing conservation actions and raising awareness of the importance of conserving nature and conserving natural resources.
